Here you can check all of my University Notes for free. I have always thought that tuition should be as accessible as possible, and for this reason I started making and sharing these notes for free. I am the author of the notes, but the material itself is taken directly from the lesson I have been following in the past years. Becuase of these last reasons, the Notes are open source and include the name of the Lecturer and the size of the file. You can download the LaTex folder, the italian version, and soon the english version as well.

These documents were writeen mostly by one or two people, and for this reason they probably contain some mistakes. Please feel free to contact me if you encounter these errors. Furthermore, feel free to better the notes themself, by adding sections, graphs, parts, design elements, translating in other languages and everything you think is necessary. I will then upload the new versions including you name.
